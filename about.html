Founder Story

Communication can be a form of service.

Francine Marie Bautista built SENZ Strategic Communications for people, brands, organizations, and communities that need more than visibility. They need direction, structure, story, and public understanding.

Her work sits at the intersection of branding, public relations, storytelling, culture, identity, visibility, public perception, creative direction, and digital strategy. But beyond titles, her work is grounded in service.

She believes a clear message can help a small business become trusted, a strong story can help a community feel seen, and a thoughtful identity can help a cultural project survive beyond the moment.

As a transwoman in the Philippines, Francine understands that image can be judged before identity is understood. That lived lesson shaped her belief that presence should carry dignity, discipline, and purpose. A public image should not only look polished; it should help people recognize the truth, care, and standard behind the name.

Through SENZ Strategic Communications, she brings together strategy, creativity, public relations, digital systems, photography, education, cultural storytelling, and community-centered work to help people and ideas become clearer, sharper, and harder to ignore.
