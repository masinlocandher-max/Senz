FAQ

Questions? We've got answers.

What does SENZ Strategic Communications do?

SENZ Strategic Communications helps brands with strategy, communications, digital presence, creative direction, and public visibility.

How does the process work?

We start with your current brand situation, clarify your goal, then design a strategy and execution plan.

Who do you work with?

We work with brands, entrepreneurs, organizations, public-facing personalities, and pageant talents.

How long does a project take?

It depends on the scope. Smaller strategy work can be faster, while full brand or digital projects need a longer timeline.
