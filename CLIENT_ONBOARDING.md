# SENZ Client Onboarding Process

## Step 1: Discovery
Understand the client's goals, challenges, audience, and desired outcomes.

## Step 2: Strategy
Develop the communication direction, creative approach, and project plan.

## Step 3: Proposal
Present scope, timeline, deliverables, and investment.

## Step 4: Production
Execute creative, digital, marketing, PR, or communication solutions.

## Step 5: Review & Delivery
Finalize outputs and ensure quality standards.

## Step 6: Growth Partnership
Continue improving brand presence through strategic support.
