Frequently Asked Questions

What responsible PR can and cannot do.

Does SENZ guarantee media coverage?

No responsible PR practitioner can guarantee independent editorial coverage. SENZ prepares the strategy, story, materials, outreach direction, and media readiness needed to improve the opportunity.

Can SENZ help during a reputation issue?

Yes. SENZ can assess narrative risk, stakeholders, available evidence, response options, and communication sequence. Legal issues should also be reviewed by qualified legal counsel.

Do you write press releases and statements?

Yes, when those materials are strategically appropriate and included in the approved scope.
