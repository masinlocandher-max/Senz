Frequently Asked Questions

What to know before you begin.

Is personal branding only for influencers?

No. It is useful for any professional whose reputation, expertise, leadership, or public visibility affects opportunities and trust.

Will SENZ manage my social media?

SENZ can design the strategy and content system. Ongoing execution may be scoped separately.

Can SENZ rewrite my biography and profiles?

Yes. Professional biography, website copy, social profile language, speaking introductions, and media materials can be included.
