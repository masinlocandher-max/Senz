Privacy Notice

How SENZ handles website information.

This notice explains the information collected through senzpr.com, why it is used, and the choices available to people who contact SENZ.

Last updated: July 10, 2026

This is a practical website privacy notice based on SENZ's current site and inquiry process. It should be reviewed whenever SENZ changes its forms, database, analytics, vendors, or business operations.

1. Scope

This notice applies to information submitted through this website, including consultation requests, general inquiries, partnership or media messages, and creative network submissions. It does not replace any separate privacy terms included in a signed client, supplier, contractor, or employment agreement.

2. Information we may collect

Depending on the form you use, SENZ may collect:

  • Your name, email address, phone number, or preferred contact details.
  • Your business, organization, role, project type, goals, timeline, and message.
  • Portfolio links, professional information, and creative-network submissions.
  • Basic technical information such as date and time of submission, browser or device information, and network address used for security and troubleshooting.

Please do not submit passwords, payment-card information, government identification numbers, medical information, or other highly sensitive data through the public website forms.

3. How information is used

SENZ may use submitted information to:

  • Review and respond to inquiries.
  • Assess project fit, scope, availability, and appropriate services.
  • Prepare a requested consultation, proposal, or follow-up.
  • Review creative profiles for relevant project-based opportunities.
  • Protect the website, investigate errors, and prevent misuse.
  • Maintain reasonable business and communication records.

SENZ does not treat a form submission as a confirmed appointment, accepted project, job offer, or signed service agreement.

4. Sharing and service providers

SENZ does not sell personal information submitted through this website. Information may be processed by trusted providers that support website hosting, database storage, email, security, communications, or technical operations. Information may also be disclosed when reasonably necessary to comply with a lawful request, protect rights or safety, investigate misuse, or complete a business transition.

5. Retention and security

SENZ keeps information only for as long as reasonably needed for the purpose it was collected, legitimate business records, dispute prevention, or applicable obligations. Creative-network profiles may be retained for future relevant opportunities unless removal is requested.

Reasonable safeguards are used, but no website, email, database, or internet transmission can be guaranteed completely secure. For confidential or highly sensitive matters, request a more appropriate communication channel before sharing details.

6. Your choices

You may contact SENZ to ask whether your submitted information is held, request a correction, withdraw a creative-network profile, or request deletion where reasonably and legally possible. SENZ may need to verify the request and may retain limited information when required for legitimate records, security, or dispute prevention.

7. Third-party links

The website may link to social platforms or other external sites. Those services have their own privacy practices, and SENZ does not control how they collect or use information.

8. Children

This website is intended for business and professional inquiries and is not designed to knowingly collect information from children. A parent or guardian who believes a child submitted information may contact SENZ to request review and removal.

9. Changes to this notice

SENZ may update this notice when the website, forms, providers, or business practices change. The latest version and update date will be posted on this page.

10. Contact

Questions or requests about this notice may be sent to info.senz.pr@gmail.com.
