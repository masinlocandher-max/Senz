Frequently Asked Questions

What to know before you begin.

Is brand strategy the same as logo design?

No. Brand strategy defines the position, audience, promise, difference, and decision framework. A logo and visual identity express that strategy.

Can SENZ work with an existing logo?

Yes. SENZ can assess whether the existing identity supports the strategy and recommend what should be retained, refined, or rebuilt.

What do I receive?

The agreed scope may include a diagnostic, positioning framework, audience definition, value proposition, messaging system, brand architecture, and creative direction.
