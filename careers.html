How It Works

A network, not a promise of employment.

01 / SUBMIT

Share your strongest work

Send a concise profile, portfolio link, role, location, and the type of projects you handle best.

02 / REVIEW

SENZ assesses fit

Profiles are reviewed based on skill, reliability, relevance, quality, and project requirements.

03 / INVITE

Relevant people are contacted

When a suitable project arises, SENZ may request availability, rates, samples, or a short conversation.

Before You Submit

Show judgment, not just volume.

A smaller portfolio of strong, relevant work is better than a large collection with no clear specialty. Please do not send confidential client materials without permission.

Be specific

State what you do, what you do best, and what kind of work you want.

Be verifiable

Use a portfolio or professional profile that clearly shows your own contribution.

Be reachable

Provide an active email and, when useful, a preferred contact channel.
